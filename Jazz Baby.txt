Brother’s hands tap.
Sister’s hands snap.
Itty-bitty Baby’s hands
CLAP-CLAP-CLAP!
Grandpa TOOT-TOOTS.
Granny sings scat.
Bitty-boppin’ Baby goes
RAT-TAT-TAT!
Mama sings high.
Daddy sings low.
Snazzy-jazzy Baby says, 
“GO, MAN, GO!”
So they TOOT-TOOT-TOOT
and they SNAP-SNAP-SNAP
and the bouncin’ baby bebops 
with a CLAP-CLAP-CLAP!
Auntie top-taps.
Uncle soft-shoes.
Fancy-dancin’ Baby sings,
“DOO-WOP-DOO!”
Cousins BOOM-BOOM.
Neighbors hip-hop.
Rompin’-stompin’ Baby goes
BOP-BOP-BOP!
Mama swings high.
Daddy swings low.
Swingin’-singin’ Baby says,
“GO, MAN, GO!”
So they BOOM-BOOM-BOOM
and they HIP-HIP-HOP
and the bouncin’ baby boogies
with a BOP-BOP-BOP!
Drummers BEAT-BEAT.
Bass players strum.
Snappy-happy Baby goes
RUM-TUM-TUM!
Tempo goes up.
Lights go down.
Busy-dizzy Baby goes round and round!
Daddy jumps high.
Mama bends low.
Laughin’-limbo Baby says, 
“GO, MAN, GO!”
So they BEAT-BEAT-BEAT
and they 
STRUM-STRUM-STRUM
and the bouncin’ baby limbos
with a RUM-TUM-TUM!
Brother’s arms hold.
Sister’s arms hug.
Rock-a-byin’ Baby 
so warm and snug.
Grandpa smiles wide.
Granny smiles, too.
Drowsy-dozy Baby sings,
“LAA-LAA-LOO.”
Daddy sings blues.
Mama sings sweet. 
While that snoozy-woozy baby…
…sleeps deep, deep, deep.
